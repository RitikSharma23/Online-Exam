  • What is the Online Exam System?

Online Examination System is a simplify way for examination activities like defining exam patterns
with question banks,defining exam timer, objective/ subjective question sections, conducting
exams using the computer or mobile devices in apaperless manner.



  • What is the role and importance of the Online Exam System?

The purpose of the online examination system is to test the subject knowledge of the students.
Such a system eliminates logistical hassles and drawbacks in the traditional mode of the pen-and-paper examination. Students don't have to assemble in the classroom to give the exam.
They can do it within a given time frame from their own device. You don't have to rent a classroom.



  • What are the benefits of the Online Exam System?

-It saves paper. You never have to print an exam for your students and hand them out.


-It saves time. You can set up an exam in such a way that it will auto-grade itself.
